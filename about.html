About me

I have been working with PHP for about 12 years and professionally for the last 8 of them. I have never been to university and am completely self taught. I don't believe you need a degree to work in this fine industry I have been lucky enough to have found myself in.

After working for several web design agencies I decided to make the switch to freelancing in 2011. TL;DR: It didn't work out.

I started working full time with the infamous PHP framework, Magento in May 2012. Magento and I had a love hate relationship and I'm sure many other Magento engineers claim the same. Unfortunately it was probably 80:20 in favour of hate.

Towards the end of 2012 I was tasked with streamlining the deployment process. This led me on a path to Gitlab which has further introduced me to Ruby. I have also started working with Ruby, and Rails in particular on a side project and have been finding it a true joy to work with. I am also trying out Chef with Vagrant as much as I can for testing and deployment.

I have just started working full time at Windsor Telecom, working on a variety of applications for clients in the in the Telecoms industry. I am very excited about this role and am looking forward to sharing my experiences with you all.

You can find me in many places online, usually under the handle m4tthumphrey.
