About me

I have been working with PHP for about 14 years and professionally for the last 8 of them. I have never been to university and am completely self taught. I don't believe you need a degree to work in this fine industry I have been lucky enough to have found myself in.

After working for several web design agencies I decided to make the switch to freelancing in 2011. TL;DR: It didn't work out.

I started working full time at Screen Pages with the infamous PHP framework in May last year. Magento and I have a love hate relationship and I'm sure many other Magento engineers claim the same. Unfortunately it is probably 80:20 in favour of hate. This is one of main reasons for this blog, so I can share my experiences with Magento, for good and for bad.

Whilst working at Screen Pages I completed the Magento Developer Certfication. I have strong views on certification exams, the MDC in particular.

Towards the end of 2012 I was tasked with streamlining our deployment process. This led me on a path to Gitlab which has further introduced me to Ruby. I have also started working with Ruby, and Rails in particular on a side project and have been finding it a true joy to work with.
